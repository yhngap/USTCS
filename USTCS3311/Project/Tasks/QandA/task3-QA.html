Q: Can we assume that if a group is assigned to an FYP, it cannot indicate an interest in any other FYP?
A: Yes. This only makes sense, right?

Q: Can a group that does not meet the minStudent or maxStudent requirement of an FYP still indicate their interest in it?
A: Since the minimum and maximum number of students is a requirement to do an FYP, the answer is no. A group must meet the min and max student group requirement to indicate an interest in an FYP.
