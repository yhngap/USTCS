IMPORTANT NOTES FOR LECTURE EXERCISES

Lecture exercises count toward the Exercises grade of the course.

Each student (or pair of students where an exercise is done with a partner)
must upload a completed exercise to their own Canvas account.
Where an exercise is completed with a partner, the exercise needs to be
uploaded to only one of the Canvas accounts of the partners.
Uploading exercises for multiple students to one Canvas account is not acceptable.

Only PDF file format and at most two upload attempts for each exercise are allowed.
Moreover, Canvas recommends that exercise file names be plain English only,
not include any special characters and not exceed half the length of the allowed input.

Late submissions and submissions by email will not be accepted.
