COURSE REQUIREMENTS AND GRADING SCHEME

  Requirement Remarks Value
1. Lecture, Tutorial and Lab Exercises   10%
2. Project   30%
  Task 1 - Entity-Relationship Schema Design 10% of course grade  
  Task 2 - Relational Table Creation and SQL Queries 10% of course grade  
  Task 3 - Information System Implementation 10% of course grade  
3. Midterm Test Oct. 16, 7:30 - 9:30 p.m. via Zoom   20%
4. Final Exam   40%

The midterm test and the final exam are open book, but only course material (i.e., textbook, lecture notes, tutorial notes and lab notes) are permitted. The final exam is cumulative with emphasis on the post-midterm material.

For the midterm test, no make-up test will be arranged. Thus, missing the midterm test will result in a loss of 20% of the final grade. For the final exam, a make-up exam will be arranged if (i) you are granted prior approval by the instructor due to extremely special circumstances or (ii) you have an unfavorable medical condition on the day of the examination as substantiated by a physician's medical certificate. Otherwise, being absent at the final exam will result in automatic failure of the course, according to university regulations, unless prior approval is obtained from the department head.
