IMPORTANT NOTES FOR LAB EXERCISES

Labs used for instruction on how to use Oracle and/or SQL
count toward the Exercises grade of the course.

Each student must upload a completed exercise to their own Canvas account.
Uploading exercises for multiple students to one Canvas account is not acceptable.

Only at most two upload attempts for each exercise are allowed.
Moreover, Canvas recommends that exercise file names be plain English only,
not include any special characters and not exceed half the length of the allowed input.

Late submissions and submissions by email will not be accepted.


The credit for a lab is given for completing and submitting,
via Canvas, the lab exercise by 11:00 p.m. on day of the lab.
