Instructions for Canvas Quiz Taking and Quiz Zoom Meeting

These are general instructions for the quiz for COMP 2012 on October 31 at 7pm.

Instructions:

  1. The quiz consists of 25 multiple-choice questions.
  2. The questions will be shown on screen one-at-a-time.
  3. The quiz must be completed within 1 hour 15 minutes from the time you start. Manage your pace accordingly so that you do not run out of time.
  4. The quiz will be conducted on Canvas.
  5. The quiz will be proctored via a Zoom meeting (see guidelines below) which will be recorded. In order to take the quiz, you must be logged in to the Zoom meeting.

Topics and Style

  1. The quiz will test your knowledge on the followings:
    • Topic 1: Review Example, Pointer, Reference & Const-ness
    • Topic 2: Object Initialization, Construction and Destruction
    • Topic 3: Inheritance and Polymorphism (Up to Page 82, i.e. not including Abstract Base Class)
    • Makefile is NOT included.
  2. It is closed-book, i.e. you are NOT allowed to use any textbooks and electronic devices (except the device you will use to connect to Canvas).

Guidelines & Regulations:

It is your responsibility to make sure that you adhere to the following regulations. Failure to do so may lead to your quiz being voided.

  1. First, join the meeting using your UST account connected to Zoom. Make sure that you join the meeting 15 minutes before the quiz time to make sure that your configuration works fine.
  2. You must use two different devices as follows:
    1. A laptop/desktop that you will use to connect to Canvas and take the quiz.
      Note:
      • The tablet/mobile version of Canvas does not always function properly with Canvas quizzes.
      • Chrome or Firefox is preferred.
      • If you use Safari 13.1 on Mac, disable cross-site tracking or you might not be able to view images in questions.
      • Update your preferred Web browser to the latest version.
      • If you are having any other issues, you may try to clear the cache.
    2. Another device (mobile phone/tablet/laptop/desktop) that you will use to join the Zoom meeting.
  3. Device (b) must have video and audio capabilities and you must make sure that its camera clearly shows your face (at least partially), your hands, and desk area so that we can make sure you are working on the Canvas quiz on your own. The screen of device (a) (i.e. the device where you are taking the Canvas quiz) must also be visible. However, the camera does not need to show your quiz input but just show enough to demonstrate thay the only active window is Canvas. See here for an example of "correct" configuration.
  4. If you have your camera turned off or not correctly configured, you will not be allowed to take the quiz and there will not be any make-up quiz for you.
  5. You are NOT allowed to access any other programs, applications, browser tabs, or windows on device (a), except for the Canvas window for taking the quiz. Keep in mind that Canvas tracks when you switch to a different window, application, or tab and we will check this.
  6. While taking the quiz, you must be alone in the room and no one else should be with you. If you are unable to have access to such a room, then you must inform the instructor of this at least two days before the quiz, explain why this is not possible and state who else might be in the room with you during the quiz.
  7. During the quiz, you may be asked to adjust your camera if it is not well-placed or to show that you are the only person in the room so make sure that you have audio turned on in device (b), but don't put on your earphones or headsets. During the exam, you must remain silent and are not allowed to communicate verbally, electronically or in any other way with anyone, except for the invigilator.
  8. If you have a question about the exam, you may click "raise hand" button. Then, the invigilator would take you to a breakout room and address the question through verbal communications.
  9. Have your HKUST student ID and HKID / passport ready at any time. You will be moved to a breakout room along to show your IDs.
  10. You are allowed to have blank sheets of paper that you will use for rough work.
